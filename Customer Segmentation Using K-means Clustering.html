Thus we have 5 distinct groups of customers purchasing from the mall. Also their centroids are marked with 'cyan'.

Final conclusion: We can say that the cluster marked with red colored points have low annual income yet their spending score is high. The cluster marked with brown colored points have low annual income and hence their spending score is low which seems to be normal. The cluster marked with yellow colored points have high annual income hence their spending score is high which also seems to be normal. The cluster marked with green colored points have low annual income and their spending score is moderate. But for the cluster marked with blue points have high annual income yet their spending score is low. Therefore the shopping mall needs to come up with a plan for the customer in blue cluster so that the customer belonging to the blue cluster spend more in the mall.
